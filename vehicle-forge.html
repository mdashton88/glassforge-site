What We're Building

A free, browser-based vehicle construction tool for Savage Worlds. Build anything from a bicycle to a battleship to a battle station — set your size, choose a locomotion type, adjust six sliders, bolt on weapons and modifications, and get a complete stat block ready for the table in minutes.

No login, no subscription, no nonsense. Just a tool built by GMs, for GMs.

What to Expect

Vehicle Forge will launch as a free download on DriveThruRPG, alongside expansion packs covering everything from Age of Sail warships and WW2 armour to post-apocalyptic war rigs, giant mecha, and deep-space capital fleets. Every vehicle comes with a complete Savage Worlds stat block, prose description, and GM notes.

The tool exports to Fantasy Grounds, Foundry VTT, and a clean printable stat block for the table. It works entirely in your browser — no installation, no cloud account, no data collection.
